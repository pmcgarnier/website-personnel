Hello, I am a multidisciplinary graphic designer who mainly work within web and editorial design.
Having a particular interest for print, fashion and culture, I’m graduate with a Master Degree.

BÂTI

Visual identity & Editorial work for Bâti, a contemporary magazine dealing with architecture and typography.

FLOU

Flou is an editorial project raising awareness of transhumanism and artificial intelligence. It teaches us about a subject that seems vague and questioning our future as humans with the arrival of machines & NBIC in our daily lives.

MONDES IMAGINAIRES

'Mondes Imaginaires' is an illustration project on the design of an idyllic and mysterious outdoor environment.

SENSE OF …

SENSE OF allows the design of workspaces as an architectural structure to be approached from another angle, which is the one of the senses. Through the study of colors, dimensions, light, energy, SENSE OF works to intelligently optimize the design of these places.

META
MORPHOSIS

Visual identity and packaging for Metamorphosis, a technological & ecological capsule collection that emerged from the collaboration between Tesla and Mugler.

EDO

Visual identity and packaging of EDO, a foodtruck aiming at satisfying a taste for truth, authenticity, through the selection of quality products (origin, traceability, quality, organic...) and a "home" cuisine, prepared on-site.

LA CÂLE

Creation of the website of "La Câle", a sail transport company linking Martinique to the Bordeaux region. Thanks to a participative economy, it delivers by the force of the wind, a selection of typical products from Martinique and Bordeaux.

UTOPIA

Motion design for "Le prix découverte des spectateurs de l'Utopia", a teaser to motivate spectators to vote for their favorite French movie.
