BÂTI

Visual identity & Editorial work for Bâti, a contemporary magazine dealing with architecture and typography.

FLOU

Flou is an editorial project raising awareness of transhumanism and artificial intelligence. It teaches us about a subject that seems vague and questioning our future as humans with the arrival of machines & NBIC in our daily lives.

MONDES IMAGINAIRES

'Mondes Imaginaires' is an illustration project on the design of an idyllic and mysterious outdoor environment.

SENSE OF …

SENSE OF allows the design of workspaces as an architectural structure to be approached from another angle, which is the one of the senses. Through the study of colors, dimensions, light, energy, SENSE OF works to intelligently optimize the design of these places.
