ABOUT ME

Passionate about visual arts, I am a Master 2 student in art direction and interactive design. Versatile, I develop refined holistic design solutions and advocate simplicity and clarity in order to stimulate the senses, impact and seduce. Mixing innovation and know-how, I make room for poetry in a contemporary universe.

I am a multidisciplinary art director who mainly works in web and editorial design. With a deep interest in architecture, fashion and culture, I will soon graduate with a Master's degree.

5 years of studies at ECV Bordeaux and my exchange semester at UQAM's design school in Montreal have allowed me to perfect my skills in various disciplines:

ART DIRECTION | BRANDING | EDITORIAL DESIGN | WEB DESIGN | UX/UI | MOTION DESIGN | TYPOGRAPHY | ILLUSTRATION | IMAGE MAKING
