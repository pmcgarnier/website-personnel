BORDEAUX LEGENDS

Branding & Illustration
Designed at ECV Bordeaux
2018

Creation of the identity of the wine bottles of Bordeaux red, Bordeaux rosé and Bordeaux white wines. We worked on the theme of the legend. Like wine, legends are timeless, They are transmitted from generation to generation and are part of the conviviality moments. Being interested in legends is a playful way to highlight Bordeaux wines and thus create a legend around its wines. We have decided to highlight 3 legends of Bordeaux through illustrations and stories.
